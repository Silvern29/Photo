Haus Himmelreich

The house

I started photography for our own house with holiday apartments. I did the pictures of the house and the rooms. I quickly changed my gear to a full frame camera Nikon D800 and some acceptable lenses for Zoom, Wideangle and Macro, but also some fixed lengths like a 50mm lense.

The hiking and ski tours

To give our guests an idea of how it is to live in the Kleinwalsertal, I take my camera with me on most of our hiking tours with the dogs and the kids.

The people

I do only a little people photography. Mostly of course for the family.

Das Naturhotel Chesa Valisa

The house

I shot most of the pictures of the Naturhotel, while working at the reception. I updated the pictures of all old and new rooms and the house itself from outside during the different seasons.

The SPA

It's really fun to make pictures of an outside SPA area during winter.

The restaurant & events

My pictures were mostly of the restaurant prepared for dinner and large events like christmas, new year or carnival.

My Hobby

Night

I managed to take some decent shots of the milky way. It's great to be outside at night trying to take a photo of stars you hardly can see by yourself.

Macro

Next comes the macro photography. You almost can't see anything. But the camera gets every little hair of an insect.

Landscape

Last but not least! I love the landscape of the Kleinwalsertal during every season. It's fantastic during winter covered in fresh snow. And a great contrast in summer with its green woods and mountains.
